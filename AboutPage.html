Karleen Dhindsa

Karleen Dhindsa is a junior graphic designer.She is fueled by her passion for creative new ideas and revising old ideas to the modern world. With her education from George Brown College for graphic design as well as Sheridan College for Creative and Visual Arts,she has a wide range of skill sets in the art and design world.Karleen considers herself a ‘forever student,’ eager to both build on her academic foundations in design and stay in tune with the latest digital updates through continued coursework as she is currently studying at George Brown College. Karleen believes teamwork, trust,communication and reliability in the workplace is key to success.
